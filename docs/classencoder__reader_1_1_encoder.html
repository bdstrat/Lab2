Detailed Description

This class allows a motor encoder to be read continuously and without over/underflow.

The motor can be spun by hand or with our motor_driver.py file.

Parameters
pinA: pin B6 or C6; encoder channel A. With encoder channel B, motor speed and position can be calculated
pinB: pin B7 or C7; encoder channel B. With encoder channel A, motor speed and position can be calculated
timer: timer with a specified period equal to the autoreload value.
chan_A: channel reading one encoder signal (A)
chan_B: channel reading the other encoder signal (B)
